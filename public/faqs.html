Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 How is my data encrypted?

We use AES-128 GCM encryption with a unique key and IV (Initialization Vector) for each entry. This ensures that even if the database is compromised, your sensitive data remains secure.

👤 Who can access my data?

Only you can access your data after logging in. Even our servers store only encrypted information. We cannot read your secrets.

🔑 What if I forget my password?

You can reset your password securely using your registered email address. For your protection, we cannot recover your original password.

📊 Are my links tracked?

Only anonymous analytics such as click counts and time of access are collected. We never sell or share personal data with third parties.

🛡️ Are shared links safe?

Yes, each shared link is protected with optional passwords and expires after a set time (if configured). This prevents unauthorized access.
