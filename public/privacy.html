Privacy Policy

Last updated August 17 2025

This policy describes how VaultX collects uses and protects your information when you use our products and services.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as username email and password
  • Service data encrypted vault items stored and managed through your account
  • Technical data such as device type browser logs and IP address collected to secure and improve the service
  • Support information such as messages you send to our team

How we use information

  • Provide operate and secure the service
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Respond to support requests and improve features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal bases where applicable

We rely on performance of a contract to provide the service legitimate interests to maintain and protect our platform and compliance with legal duties. Where consent is required we will request it and you may withdraw it at any time.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in protect your session and analyze basic usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings though certain features may not work without them.

Data retention

We retain account information for as long as you maintain an account. You may delete your account at any time using the account settings which also deletes your related vault items subject to backup retention for a short period.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the platform under duty of confidentiality and only as needed. We may disclose information to comply with law or protect rights safety and security.

Security

Vault items are stored encrypted at rest. We apply technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No service can be perfectly secure and you share responsibility for safeguarding your credentials.

Your rights

Depending on your location you may have rights to access correct delete or export your information and to object to or restrict certain processing. To make a request contact us using the details below.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in other countries that may have different data protection laws. We take steps to protect your information consistent with this policy.

Children

VaultX is not intended for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If changes are material we will provide notice by posting an update on this page or by email when appropriate.
